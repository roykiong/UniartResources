About us

UNIART RESOURCES was established in 2020 as a marketing arm of Wintage Group Sdn Bhd which has more than 20 years of substantial experience in manufacturing of custom-built residential and commercial furniture, including government projects.
We aims to providing the premium quality cabinetry offerings ranging from low end to high end that fit your needs, desire, budget, and styleseamlessly.

As a professional solution provider for customize furniture in Malaysia, we are take ample information from customers, so that we are able to fully understand your needs and wishes before kick-start the cabinet works. Your dreams and ideas are then well incorporated into your kitchen. We believe when your needs are matched, you are excited to enjoy your life to the fullest and every moment in your dream house.
